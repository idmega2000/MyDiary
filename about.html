About Diary

A Diary is a record that can be used to detail everything from your feelings about a particular situation in your social life to your thoughts on a current event in the political world. Hence, journals serve a range of purposes. A Diary is meant to save your thoughts, ideas and observations on any thing and allows you to put the happenings of each day into writing. This gives you the opportunity to remember what you thought about and what happened some time past. it allow you to remember what things happened when you were younger

There are many types of diaries, and many choose to share different things. The usual definition is that a diary a dated record of what's happened. Having it online provides many improvements to the old hand-written type.

  • It's more secure, since it requires login credentials to use. No longer will siblings, parents or spouses snoop in private diaries.
  • You can set reminder to get notification reminding you of putting down your thoughts
  • You can optionally share your diary details
